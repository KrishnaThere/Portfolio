Hi! I’m Krishna. Thank you for visiting my site and exploring my work.

I'm a girl from Gujarat, India, with a dream to embrace everything life has to offer. In my early high school years, I was on a clear path toward engineering, but the artist within me eventually took the lead. My dad’s transfer to the United States became a blessing in disguise, guiding me to one of the most celebrated design schools—Parsons School of Design.

Parsons has molded me into a very confident designer by exposing me to the vibrant New York City and extremely tallented and inspiring artists from every part of the globe to collaborte with. Being part of a global design community has offered me invaluable opportunities to collaborate with diverse minds and explore multiple perspectives and ideologies, profoundly enriching both my work and my personal growth.

I have a bunch of hobbies; the list includes but is not limited to: solving sudoku, hicking, dancing(also am really good at it), music(can't play any instruments or sing just have love for it), painting and many more.

To me, design is about more than just presenting complex concepts in a simplified and accessible way. It's about creating experiences that evoke strong, lasting emotions and provoke thought, leaving a meaningful impact on those who engage with it.
